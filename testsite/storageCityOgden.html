About

Storage City of Ogden is located north of the Ogden airport off of Hinckley Drive. Your Odgen storage unit is strickly month to month, no long leases needed. Your storage space rental starts the day you move in. There is a $20 deposit which is refundable. You may provide your own lock or purchase one from us at $6 - $12. Visit us at http://ogdenstoragefacility.com

Our facility gate is secured with a computerized access panel, which accepts your personalized security code. Once inside our Ogden facility only your can access your storage unit, because only you have the key to your personal storage space. Our friendly resident managers would be happy to discuss what storage unit is right for you and your needs. (This is a DEA dog sniffing training facility after hours)
