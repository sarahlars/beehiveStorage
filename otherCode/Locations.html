About

We are located north of the Heber City airport. At our Heber facility you will find a variety of self storage units. Our friendly on-site resident managers would be happy to discuss what storage units is right for you and your needs.

Our storage facility gate is secured with a computerized access panel, which accepts your personalized security code. Once inside our facility only you can access your storage unit because only you have the key to your personal storage space. You may provide your own lock or purchase one from us for $6 - $12.
